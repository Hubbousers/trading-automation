PHASE 3: RIGOROUS BACKTESTING ENGINE + STRUCTURE DETECTION (Weeks 7-9)
================================================================================
Goal: Build production-grade backtesting system (NO live trading without this)

3.1 Professional Backtesting Engine
   - Event-driven backtester (simulates market in real-time)
   - Accurate transaction cost modeling:
     * Brokerage fees (0.05% typical for Angel One)
     * Bid-ask spread (0.01-0.05% depending on liquidity)
     * Slippage (price movement during order execution)
   - Position sizing calculations
   - Realistic order execution simulation
   - Trade logging and analytics
   
3.2 Market Structure Detection Algorithms (YOUR CORE EDGE!)
   Build automated detectors for your edge:
   
   3.2.1 Swing High/Low Identification
      - Algorithm: Find local maxima/minima in price data
      - Input: OHLC data + period (e.g., 5 candles)
      - Output: List of swing points with timestamps
      - Use for: Building structure foundation
   
   3.2.2 Break of Structure (BOS) Detection
      - Algorithm: Compare current price vs previous swing levels
      - Uptrend BOS: Price > previous swing high + Volume confirmation
      - Downtrend BOS: Price < previous swing low + Volume confirmation
      - Signal: Strong directional move likely coming
      - Code it: If price_break_swing AND volume_high â†’ BOS detected
   
   3.2.3 Change of Character (CHOCH) Detection
      - Algorithm: Identify trend reversal points
      - Uptrend CHOCH: Lower High + Lower Low after higher lows
      - Downtrend CHOCH: Higher Low + Higher High after lower highs
      - Signal: Trend ending, position should close
      - Code it: Detect pattern in last 3-5 candles
   
   3.2.4 Liquidity Zone Mapping
      - Algorithm: Identify past support/resistance levels
      - Input: Historical swing points (previous 100+ candles)
      - Output: Liquidity zones (price levels revisited multiple times)
      - Use for: Entries (buy near support) / Exits (sell near resistance)
      - Code it: Count touches of each price level, cluster by density
   
   3.2.5 Internal Day Movement (IDM) Pattern Recognition
      - Algorithm: Analyze intraday consolidation/breakout patterns
      - Inside bar: High < previous High AND Low > previous Low
      - Breakout: Price breaks consolidation with volume
      - Use for: Intraday entries and scalping opportunities
   
3.3 Comprehensive Performance Metrics
   - Total return, annualized return, cumulative returns
   - Sharpe ratio, Sortino ratio, Calmar ratio, Omega ratio
   - Maximum drawdown and recovery time
   - Win rate, profit factor, average win/loss
   - Consecutive wins/losses
   - Ulcer index, recovery factor
   - NEW: Metrics specific to structure-based trading:
     * Success rate of BOS entries (% profitable)
     * Average profit on BOS trades
     * CHOCH accuracy (how often it signals reversal correctly)
   
3.4 Rigorous Testing Methodology
   - Walk-forward analysis (out-of-sample validation)
     * Train on 2 years â†’ Test on 3 months
     * Roll forward weekly, never look ahead
   - Out-of-sample testing (minimum 3 months unseen data)
   - Monte Carlo simulation (1000+ iterations):
     * Test if strategy is robust or overfitted
     * Check drawdown scenarios
   - Stress testing:
     * 2008 financial crisis
     * Flash crashes (May 6, 2010)
     * COVID crash (March 2020)
     * Gap scenarios
   
3.5 Documentation Requirements
   - Hypothesis: Why does market structure predict price moves?
   - Methodology: Exact rules for BOS, CHOCH, IDM detection
   - Statistical tests: Confidence intervals, p-values
   - Backtest results with confidence bands
   - Risk analysis: Max loss scenarios
   - Why it might fail: Market regime changes, choppy conditions
